Welcome to Lucky Joe's Sidewalk Saloon

Northern Colorado's home of free live music and home cookin. No matter where you're from we are your neighborhood bar!

We like to think of Lucky Joe’s

as a friendly Irish Pub in an Old Western Saloon. Relax and be our guests for a fun and casual dining experience. Starting with the finest, fresh ingredients that we can find, we home-cook them with pride and care for a wonderful lunch, dinner or snack. We feature an extensive list of beers from very near and very far, a thoughtful wine selection and cocktails for kids of all ages. Combined with our Old Town atmosphere and Old-Fashioned hospitality, you can’t help but have a Good Ole Time at Lucky Joe's.
